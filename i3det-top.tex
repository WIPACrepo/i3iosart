\title{The Instrumentation and Online Systems \\
  of the IceCube Neutrino Observatory}

\author{[author list here]}

\abstract{The IceCube Neutrino Observatory is a cubic-kilometer-scale
  neutrino detector built into the glacial ice at the South Pole.  We
  describe here the detector instrumentation, including the digital optical
  modules (DOMs), cable systems, and computing hardware, along with
  our methodology for drilling and deployment.  The online triggering and
  data filtering systems select candidate neutrino and cosmic ray events
  from the background dark noise.  Varied techniques are presented for
  calibrating DOM waveforms and optical efficiency.  Due to a rigorous
  pre-deployment testing protocol, as well as an emphasis on software
  stability and monitoring, IceCube routinely achieves an uptime of greater
  than 99\%.}

\keywords{neutrino detectors, trigger concepts and systems (hardware and
  software), online farms and online filtering}
